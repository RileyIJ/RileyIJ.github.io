About Me

Howdy! I’m Riley Isaiah Johnson.

I’m an a second year Electrical Engineering and Science student at Monash University in Melbourne, although I’m originally from a small town in Western Victoria called Casterton. I made the move to Melbourne at the start of 2021 to pursue my studies and moved to the Logan hall residence on campus. In my time here I’ve tken on a number of roles including; the Logan hall student society’s Vice-president/treasurer, a residential advisor of Logan hall, and the advocacy officer of the Monash Residential Services residence committee.

This website serves as a place for me to put all of my creative projects and things I work on and make both in and outside of my university studies. I’ve got a lot of ideas for what I can do here, so keep an eye out for new posts to keep up to date with what I get up to :)
